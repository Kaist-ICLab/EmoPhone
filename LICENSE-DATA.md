# Data License and Access Terms

The final data license and Data Use Agreement (DUA) for the D1, D2, and D3
archives are **TBD** and will be populated before public release.

The planned release path is Harvard Dataverse with gated access. Users will be
required to agree to the project DUA before downloading the dataset. The exact
license identifier, Dataverse landing page, DOI, and file IDs remain placeholders
until acceptance/publication.

## Draft DUA Principles

The final DUA is expected to cover at least the following restrictions:

1. **No re-identification.** Users must not attempt to re-identify participants
   or infer identifying attributes from the released data.
2. **No surveillance or profiling.** Users must not use the data to target,
   monitor, profile, or surveil any individual or group.
3. **Respect anonymisation.** Users must not attempt to reverse privacy
   transformations such as contact-number hashing, Wi-Fi/Bluetooth identifier
   replacement, or GPS displacement.
4. **Redistribution limits.** Users may share aggregate analyses and models
   that do not expose participant-level data, but raw dataset redistribution
   terms are TBD.
5. **Citation.** Publications, preprints, and public presentations using the
   dataset must cite the final dataset release and accompanying paper once those
   records are available.
6. **Incident reporting.** Users should notify the maintainers promptly if they
   become aware of a privacy or rights concern involving the dataset.
