## Dataverse Data Use Agreement (additional terms)

In addition to CC BY-NC 4.0, access to the data through Harvard Dataverse requires agreeing to the project-specific Data Use Agreement. The DUA restricts use to **non-commercial academic research** and imposes the following additional conditions (summarised here; the DUA on the Dataverse page is authoritative):

1. **No re-identification.** You will not attempt to re-identify any participant or infer identifying attributes from the released data, including but not limited to cross-referencing with external datasets.
2. **No surveillance or profiling.** You will not use the data to target, profile, monitor, or otherwise surveil any individual or group.
3. **Respect anonymisation.** You will not attempt to reverse the applied anonymisation transformations (MD5-hashed contact numbers, UUID-replaced Wi-Fi/Bluetooth identifiers, per-participant random GPS longitude displacement).
4. **Redistribution.** You may redistribute derived features, models, and analyses that do not expose individual-level raw data. You may not redistribute the raw dataset files themselves outside of Harvard Dataverse; refer downstream users to the Dataverse landing page.
5. **Citation.** Any publication, preprint, or public presentation using the dataset must cite the dataset release and the NeurIPS paper (see [`CITATION.cff`](./CITATION.cff) and [`README.md`](./README.md#citation)).
6. **Incident reporting.** You will notify the maintainers promptly if you become aware of any privacy or rights violation involving the dataset.
