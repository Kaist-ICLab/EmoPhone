## Form outline (placeholder)

The final form will cover, at minimum, the following sections (this outline reflects the structure of the approved Korean form):

1. **Study title and research team.** Title of the study; names and affiliations of the Principal Investigator and research staff; contact information for the IRB office.
2. **Purpose.** A plain-language description of the research purpose (modelling momentary affective states from smartphone and wearable sensing).
3. **What participation involves.** What participants will be asked to do: install the ABC Logger Android app, respond to ESM prompts during waking hours, wear the Fitbit Inspire HR (and, for D1/D2 subgroups, the Polar H10) during the study period, and complete onboarding / exit questionnaires.
4. **Duration.** Approximate duration of the study (~30 days per wave) and expected time commitment per day.
5. **Data collected.** What data will be collected: ESM self-report labels, passive smartphone sensor streams, wearable streams, and questionnaire responses at onboarding / exit.
6. **How the data will be used.** Academic research, publications, and — with the participant's explicit consent — archival release under a gated Data Use Agreement on Harvard Dataverse for non-commercial academic research.
7. **Anonymisation.** Description of the anonymisation transformations that will be applied before release: MD5-hashed contact numbers, UUID-replaced Wi-Fi/Bluetooth identifiers, per-participant random displacement of GPS longitude, removal of structurally invalid records.
8. **Risks and benefits.** Low-to-moderate privacy risk from passive sensing; residual re-identification risk acknowledged and mitigated. Benefits are indirect (contribution to research) and direct (compensation).
9. **Compensation.** ~100 USD equivalent, provided on study completion.
10. **Confidentiality.** Access controls during the study; anonymisation and DUA-gated access for the public release; data stored on secure institutional infrastructure.
11. **Voluntary participation and right to withdraw.** Participation is voluntary; participants may withdraw at any time without consequence and may request deletion of their data.
12. **Contact information.** Contact details for the Principal Investigator, research staff, and the IRB office for questions or concerns.
13. **Signature block.** Participant's printed name, signature, and date.
